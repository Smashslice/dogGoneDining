Established in 2018, we've been serving faithful companions ever since. Ask around, anyone will tell you Dog Gone Dining is the safest coziest place for you and your pets to grab a bite.

Dogs are our passion and we love to see them happy. Have a special request or dietary need for your favorite pup? Let us know over at the Contact Page! We'll do everything we can to make sure you and your friend have a great experience.

We're looking to expand! We've been growing for 7 years and we're considering making Dog Gone Dog Park right next door. A step away and you and your furry friends could go for a jog, take to the training course or go for a dip in the Dog Gone Pond! If this sounds as amazing to you as it does to us, hop over and let us know on the Contact Page!
